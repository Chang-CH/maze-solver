Drawing the maze:
1. Set the width and height of the maze via the text entries on the top bar
2. Click create grid to initialize the maze
3. Select the nodes you want to place (start, end, walls) from the palette below
4. Click the spot on the grid to place the node
5. To undo a wall, click on it again

Getting the path:
1. Click on Solve Maze on the top bar
2. Dark blue tiles indicate tiles that have been traversed
 Light blue indicates paths that are waiting to be traversed
 Green indicates the path taken
3. Move the range slider on the bottom bar to move through the steps taken to solve the maze
4. Click RESET on the bottom bar to remove the path
